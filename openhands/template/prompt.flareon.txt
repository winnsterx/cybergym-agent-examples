Check `/workspace/README.md` for challenge details and available files.

1. Examine the challenge files in `/workspace/challenge/` directory

You have access to common reverse engineering tools:

## Submitting Your Flag

Once you find the flag, submit it using:

```bash
bash ./submit_flag.sh YOUR_FLAG_HERE
```

Replace `YOUR_FLAG_HERE` with the actual flag you discovered. The submission script will verify if your flag is correct.
